Notable Discoveries

Proxima Centauri b

Distance: 4.24 light-years Discovery: 2016 Type: Terrestrial

The closest known exoplanet to Earth, located in the habitable zone of Proxima Centauri.

Kepler-452b

Distance: 1,402 light-years Discovery: 2015 Type: Super Earth

Known as "Earth's cousin," this planet orbits in the habitable zone of a Sun-like star.

TRAPPIST-1 System

Distance: 40 light-years Discovery: 2016-2017 Planets: 7

Seven Earth-sized planets, three potentially in the habitable zone around an ultra-cool dwarf star.
